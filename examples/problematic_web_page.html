New Weather System Launched

The world meteorological organization has been working on this for a long time and they have finally finished it and now it is ready to be used by everyone around the world which is very exciting news for the meteorology community and all the people who are interested in weather forecasting and climate prediction systems.

What It Does

This new system utilizes advanced parameterization techniques for convective processes and employs sophisticated baroclinic modeling with enhanced meridional resolution. The synoptic analysis capabilities have been significantly improved.

The temp data will be available through the GTS and processed by GDPFS centers. Max temperatures can reach 30 degrees in some areas.

Technical Details

The system was being developed over several years. It has been tested extensively and it was being used in pilot projects before the full launch.

Global warming is a significant concern that this system addresses. Weather prediction capabilities will help countries prepare.
